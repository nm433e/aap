54. ね

Note: Ne is sometimes pronounced nē.
1. Indicates emotion or feelings of admiration.

きれいな花ねぇ。

What a pretty flower!

すばらしい演奏だったわね。

That was such a wonderful concert.

Note: Here, wa indicates that a woman is speaking.
2. Indicates agreement with the other person.

本当にそうですね。

Yes, that’s quite true.

おっしゃる通りですね。

Yes, it’s just as you say.

3. Softens a request.

できればぜひお願いしますね。

If possible, please be sure to do it.

必ず手紙を下さいね。

Be sure to write me a letter.

4. Indicates a request for confirmation.

あの本、持って来て下さったでしょうね。

You brought me that book, didn’t you?

原田さん、今日来ると言ったんですね。

Harada said that he’d be coming today, right?

5. Indicates a mild assertion of, or variance in, opinion.

そうですかね。

Oh, is that so?

本当にそんなこと起こったんでしょうかね。

Hmm, I wonder if that’s what actually happened.

6. Indicates a mild assertion.

あの人たち、何を考えているのかわかりませんね。

I just can’t understand what they’re thinking about.

私は北海道の方が寒いと思うんですけどね。

I would think that Hokkaido is colder.
