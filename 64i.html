64. い

1. Following da or ka, indicates an informal question. Used mostly by men.

どうして新宿まで行ったんだい。

Why’d you go as far as Shinjuku?

昨日どこで飲んだんだい。

Where’d you go drinking yesterday?

またアメリカに出張かい、大変だな。

Off to the U.S. on business again? Hang in there.

あの人、元気だったかい。

How was she?
