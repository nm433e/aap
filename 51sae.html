51. さえ

1. “Even.”
Note: This usage is similar to de mo (#5, no. 2), but is more emphatic.

その問題は先生でさえ答えられなかった。

Even the teacher couldn’t answer that question.

そんな簡単なことは、子供でさえ知っていますよ。

Even a child knows such a simple thing!

2. In the form sae…-tara or sae…-ba, implies that if something additional is (had been) done, a positive outcome will occur (would have occurred): “if only.”

もう５分さえあったら、飛行機に間に合ったのに……

If we had had only five more minutes, we would have been on time for the flight.

健ちゃんは頭がいいんですから、勉強さえすればいい大学に入れますよ。

Ken has a good head on his shoulders, so if he only studies, he’ll be able to get into a good university.
