27. もので

1. Indicates a cause or reason: “in that, inasmuch as, given.”
Note: Mono de is more polite and formal than no de and kara.

東京は物価が高いもので、生活が大変です。

Since prices are high, life in Tokyo is difficult.

私は体が弱いもので、長い旅行は無理です。

In that my constitution is weak, long trips are out of the question.
